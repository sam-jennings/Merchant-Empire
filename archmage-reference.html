ARCHMAGE ASCENSION

Quick Reference
🎴

Deck Selection

  • 2–4 players: Values 1–15 (62 cards)
  • 5–7 players: Values 1–20 (84 cards)

4 energies × values + 2 or 4 wilds

🃏

Starting Components

  • All players: 7 cards each
  • The Exchange: 5 cards face-up
  • The Vault: Remaining cards face-down
  • First player: Determined randomly
⚡

Initial Sacrifice

Before the first turn begins:

Each player simultaneously discards 1 component to Used Components

This guarantees sufficient Wild Magic for final preparations

🔮

Starting Counters

Each player takes 1 counter

Counters represent your magical capacity for:

  • Casting spells
  • Taking learning actions
🌟

The Four Elements

♠ Spades: Radiance
♥ Hearts: Void
♦ Diamonds: Flux
♣ Clubs: Aether
Each card is a magical component: Energy (suit) + Value (1–20)
🔮

Conjuration

Same Energy

Requirements

3+ components of the same energy type

Benefit When Cast

Power Generation: Draw extra components

  • 3-5 components: +1 component
  • 6+ components: +2 components
🔄

Transfiguration

Sequential Values

Requirements

3+ components in sequence (any elements)

Cannot loop around (1-20-19 invalid)
1-2-3 and 18-19-20 are valid

Benefit When Cast

Transformation Rights: Exchange with The Exchange

  • 3 components: Discard 2, take 1
  • 4+ components: Discard 1, take 1
⚡

Enchantment

Matching Values

Requirements

3-4 components of the same value

Benefit (Always Active)

Enhanced Capability:

  • 3 components: +1 learning action & +1 spell cast per turn
  • 4 components: Unlimited learning & casting
When learned: Gain +1 counter (3-card) or set counters aside (4-card)
✨

Perfect Transmutation

Sequential Same Energy

Requirements

3+ components in sequence of the same energy type

E.g., Void 8–9–10 (cannot loop)

Benefit When Cast

Ultimate Power: Grants BOTH

  • Power Generation (draw extras)
  • Transformation Rights (exchange)

Uses only 1 counter when cast!

Key Spell Concepts

Enchantments Don't Need Casting

They're always active and automatically provide their benefits

Other Spells Must Be Cast

Place counters on them at turn start to activate their benefits

Wild Cards

Can substitute for any energy type — but the value still counts for sequential spells

🎯

Turn Sequence

1. Collection

Draw 1 from The Vault or take 1 from The Exchange

2. Casting

Activate spells one at a time with counters — each effect resolves immediately

3. Learning

Counters refresh — use them to Learn, Empower, Reshape, or Unlearn

📥

1. Collection Phase

Choose one

  • Draw 1 from The Vault, OR
  • Take 1 from The Exchange (immediately replace from The Vault)
That's it — no extra draws here. Conjuration bonus draws happen during the Casting Phase when you cast the spell.
⚡

2. Casting Phase

Activate spells by placing 1 counter per spell. Effects resolve immediately — one spell at a time.

Casting Conjuration

  • 3–5 cards: draw +1 from The Vault
  • 6+ cards: draw +2 from The Vault

Casting Transfiguration

  • 3-card: discard 2 from hand → take 1 from Exchange
  • 4+-card: discard 1 from hand → take 1 from Exchange

Discarded components go to Used Components. Replace Exchange card from Vault immediately.

Can't resolve = can't cast. If you lack the cards to complete a Transfiguration exchange, you cannot cast that spell this turn.
📚

3. Learn Phase

Counters refresh — all counters return to your pool and now represent learning actions.

Actions available

  • Base: 1 action  |  Each 3-card Enchantment: +1  |  4-card Enchantment: unlimited

Actions (1 counter each)

  • LEARN: Place a valid spell from hand into Spellbook
    3-card Enchantment → gain +1 counter now; 4-card → unlimited capacity now
  • EMPOWER: Add components from hand to an existing spell (can change its spell type)
  • RESHAPE: Break down and rebuild any spells with the same components — all must remain valid
    ⚠ Breaking a 3-card Enchantment costs 1 counter; breaking a 4-card Enchantment removes unlimited capacity
  • UNLEARN: Dissolve one spell — components return to hand but cannot be learned again until next turn
    ⚠ Same counter penalties as Reshape if the spell was an Enchantment
⚡

Understanding Counters

At Turn Start

Counters represent spells you can cast

Place them on spells to activate their benefits

During Learn Phase

Same counters represent learning actions available

Use them to Learn/Empower/Reshape/Unlearn

🎲

Counter Management

Gaining Counters

  • Start: 1 counter
  • Learn 3-component Enchantment: +1 counter
  • Learn 4-component Enchantment: Unlimited (set aside counters)

Losing Counters

  • Break/Unlearn 3-card Enchantment: −1 counter
  • Break/Unlearn 4-card Enchantment: lose unlimited capacity
Keep your counters in front of you throughout the game
🎯

Strategic Casting

Example with 2 counters:

Option 1: Cast 2 Conjurations → Maximum gathering

Option 2: Cast 1 Conjuration + 1 Transfiguration → Gathering + transforming

Perfect Transmutation uses only 1 counter but grants both benefits!
💫

Enchantments Special Rules

  • Always active - no casting needed
  • Don't use counters for activation
  • Automatically boost your capacity
  • Stack with each other
3 three-component Enchantments = 4 total counters (1 base + 3 bonus)
🌊

Wild Magic Surge

When It Happens

The instant The Vault empties, Wild Magic erupts!

Why the Initial Sacrifice matters: The component each player discarded at game start guarantees there will be sufficient Wild Magic for final preparations before the Ascension Trial.
🔀

Creating Wild Magic Surge

Setup

  1. Shuffle The Exchange together with Used Components
  2. This forms the Wild Magic Surge pile
  3. Keep turn order as normal
There is no Exchange during this phase
⚡

Modified Turn Structure

Before Turn: Choose Spells to Cast

Place counters as normal, but benefits are limited (see below)

Collection Phase

Draw exactly 1 component from Wild Magic Surge

  • Ignore base acquisition options
  • Ignore all Conjuration bonuses

Casting Phase

No spell effects — casting is suspended; The Vault and Exchange are gone

Learn Phase

Unchanged - use learning actions normally

⚠️

Why Cast Spells Lose Effectiveness

When Wild Magic erupts, the fundamental structure of magical reality destabilizes:

❌ Conjurations

No bonus draws - There are no stable elemental sources to draw from; all magic is chaotic and uncontrolled

❌ Transfigurations

No transformations — The Exchange has collapsed; there's nowhere to perform careful exchanges

✅ Enchantments

Still work! - As permanent self-enhancements, Enchantments continue to boost your learning capacity and casting ability even amid chaos

You can still cast spells during this phase (place counters on them if you wish), but only Enchantments provide their normal benefits. Conjurations and Transfigurations remain inert until the trial.
🏁

Game End

When the Wild Magic Surge pile is depleted, proceed immediately to your chosen end-game method:

Option 1: Simple Scoring

Tally Recognition Points from all spells

Option 2: The Ascension Trial (Recommended)

Face six magical challenges to prove your mastery

Tiebreakers (in order)

The Ascension Trial

Recommended End Game Method

A strategic, interactive finale that rewards how you built your magical power. The wizard who demonstrates the greatest mastery across six magical challenges becomes THE Archmage.

LAUNCH ASCENSION TRIAL ⚡
The trials.html app handles all calculations automatically - power domains, allocations, tie-breakers, and winner determination. Just enter your spells and make your strategic choices!
⚗️

Overview

The Process

  1. Calculate Power from each domain based on your spells
  2. Secretly allocate Power to 6 Trials
  3. Reveal simultaneously
  4. Highest legal power wins each Trial's Recognition Points
🔥

Power Calculation

3-4 components: Power = Length
5-6 components: Power = Length × 1.5 (round normally)
7+ components: Power = Length × 2.0

Examples

3 components= 3 power
5 components= 8 power (5 × 1.5 = 7.5 → 8)
7 components= 14 power
💫

Power Domains

Energy Power

Combined power from Conjurations in each energy type

Four separate domains (Radiance, Void, Flux, Aether)

Transformation Power

Total length of all Transfiguration spells

Perfect Transmutations count as Transfigurations

Enchantment Power

Total size of all Enchantments

Sum of all Enchantment component counts

✨

Wild Magic Power

Bonus allocation flexibility:

Sources

  • Each Perfect Transmutation: +1 wild magic
  • Each 4-component Enchantment: +1 wild magic

Rules

  • Only add to trials you're already attempting (with ≥1 normal power)
  • Doesn't count as a domain source
  • Affects total only (not tie-breakers)

Tie-Breakers

When trials tie, resolve in order:

  1. Utilization: Power used ÷ Total capacity of domains used
  2. Focus: Power on this trial ÷ Total power across all domains
  3. Breadth: More distinct sources (Harmony/Universal only)
  4. Shared Recognition: Both players get full points
